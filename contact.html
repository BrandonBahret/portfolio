What recurring fees should be expected?

Web design and development services require a one-time fee; however, every website requires a recurring variable budget for things like hosting, domain registration, security certificates.

These annual fees are not determined by me, and instead determined by the selected vendors. As such, estimates are a case-by-case basis.

Expect annual fees to be at least $100/year.

How do you set your prices?

Per project; never per hour. Hourly jobs leave everyone stressed. You wouldn't want to pay more for a project because it's taking longer than anticipated, so don't! You're paying for software, not the development time that goes into it.

most projects: $200 - contact me for an estimate.

Yes; however, I can't build applications for Apple devices. Apple forces developers to buy their computers (which I cannot afford) to make software for their devices.
